INTENTION

The will to be more mindful will make all the difference. At QUA , our goal is to always create something magical, aspirational but still rooted in reality.


We’re not perfect.

But we are trying our best, and we’re sure you wish to do the same. Here’s how we try to make conscious choices, so that you, who buys from us, ultimately does the same.


We’re not racing time.
We’re all about slow fashion.

The equivalent of one garbage truck full of clothes is burned or dumped in a landfill every second.

Imagine how fast people get rid of clothes, and wear-and-tear seems to be one of the culprits. Inevitably, if companies make clothes that fast, they can’t use the best fabrics, and that’s why we stay away from producing by the minute.

Quality is the core of our philosophy and thus, we’re always looking for the best of the best. Moving away from polyester, we choose light-weight, soft-to-touch comfortable fabrics that allow you to breathe and move freely.

Picking up high-end quality premium fabrics like cotton, viscose and wool blends that allow comfort, breathability and functionality, and combining them with perfect tailoring and a dedicated workforce, our clothing came into being.

We work hard for our fabrics.
And that’s why they’ll last you a lifetime.
